About me

Services

Full Caravan Service

This is a full safety check of all the appliances gas and electric, inspection of the brakes and chassis, external and internal components and a full damp report of the caravan.

Prices start at £240 for a Single Axle caravan and £280 for a Twin Axle.

Motorhome Habitation Service

I will ensure that your home on wheels is in top-notch condition. From checking the gas and water systems to ensuring all the electrical bits are humming along. Think of it as a spa day for your caravan.

Hot showers are a must, even when you're on the move. I will swiftly tackle any water heater hiccups, so you can enjoy the luxury of a warm shower wherever your caravan takes you.

Whether it's setting up a new power system or fixing an existing one, i've got the volts covered. Trust me to handle your caravan's electrical needs with finesse and expertise.

Don't let a malfunctioning fridge spoil your on-the-road feast. I will have your fridge humming back to life, keeping your snacks cool and your drinks refreshing.

Keep the elements at bay by letting me expertly over-seal every nook and cranny of your caravan. A little TLC goes a long way in preserving your mobile haven from the unpredictable UK weather.

Your caravan's chassis is its backbone. I'll ensure it stays sturdy and reliable, ready to tackle winding roads and unexpected bumps, so you can cruise with confidence.

No need to hold it in! My quick and efficient toilet repairs will have your onboard facilities functioning flawlessly, ensuring comfort and convenience during your travels.

Embrace eco-friendly power with my solar panel installations. Let the sun work its magic and keep your caravan charged up, so you can enjoy off-grid adventures without a worry.
